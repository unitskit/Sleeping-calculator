About Sleep Cycles

Sleep occurs in cycles of about 90 minutes each. Waking up at the end of a sleep cycle helps you feel more refreshed than waking up in the middle of deep sleep.

Why It Matters

Each sleep cycle consists of four stages: three stages of non-REM sleep and one REM (rapid eye movement) sleep stage. Completing full cycles is essential for:

  • Memory consolidation
  • Physical restoration
  • Emotional regulation
  • Cognitive function

Sleep Tips

For better sleep quality:

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine
  • Keep your bedroom cool and dark
  • Avoid screens before bedtime
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol intake
